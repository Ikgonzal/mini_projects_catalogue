Our Club

All of our menu items are inspired by japanese cuisine and have been created by our head chef, Carlos Suarez, after studying authentic japanese cuisine in Tokyo. Not only do we have fresh flown-in seafood from the northeast, but we also have a variety of handcrafted cocktails, wine, and beer to choose from. Come dine with us & experience an authentic magic meal in an intimate dining space. We look forward to serving you!
